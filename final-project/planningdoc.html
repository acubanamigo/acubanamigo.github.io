Project Proposal



Project Purpose


The project I will build is a Family Home Evening website that will deliver activity ideas, spiritual thoughts/lesson ideas, and treat recipes to the audience. The intent of this website is to provide young single adults with a resource to a multitude of FHE resources at their disposal. I will have a space for lesson topics, top stories, navigation links/advertisements, and a navigation bar at the top of the page to provide the users with a seamless browsing experience.


The Audience

My audience for this website is young single adults that are members of the Church of Jesus Christ, who may even be FHE Dads/Moms who are looking for ideas they could use for their callings. Or simply any single adult that needs ideas for just about anything related to Family Home Evening.

  • Age: 18-30
  • Income: 20k or less
  • Education: Young adults of all levels of education.
  • Gender: Both male and female

Web Pages

  • Home page - This will be the home page of the website itself allowing the user to browse to the different webpages and contain the content video for the particular webpage itself.
  • FHE lesson topics - This will contain lesson topic ideas and lesson plans
  • Education: Young adults of all levels of education.
  • Activity ideas - This webpage will contain activity ideas for the target audience
  • Treat recipes - This webpage will contain treat recipes for FHE
  • An About section
  • And also a Contact Us section
